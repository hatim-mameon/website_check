Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/02/2021

By working with and using mameon.com, you agree to the collection and use of your information as stated in this policy. If any changes are made to our privacy policy we will notify you via email and update it on this page as well.

  • We will never give, sell, rent or borrow your information to anyone.
  • We will never spam you.
  • We will never have direct access to your payment methods without your consent.
  • You can opt out at any time.
  • You can request access to your data at any time.

How We Treat Your Information

We do not share your information

We will not share your personal data with any third parties without your explicit permission. Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services.

We will not spam you

We may send you account updates, correspondence related to your account, blog posts, and newsletters. You can unsubscribe from any non-essential communications at any time.

Payment processing

All payments are processed through secure external payment processors. We never store or have direct access to your payment information.

We use your information for personalization, improved service delivery, and transaction processing. We will never sell your data.

We may collect the following types of information:

This information is collected through registration forms and business strategy consultations.

How We Protect Your Information

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age.

This privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and social advertising platforms, not to information collected offline.
