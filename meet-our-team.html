Meet Our Team

We're a small crew with big hearts, and bigger ambition. We want to make storytelling effortless and we're just getting started!

JASON O'MAHONY

Founder & CEO

Jason was born on the wild Atlantic coast of Ireland, and is a master storyteller.

He's made documentaries for PBS, was Head of Production at Udacity, and is a graduate of Stanford GSB.

He wants to build a world where every startup can connect and communicate with their customers as effectively as the biggest brands.

WAYNE GAO

CTO

With over 20 years of experience in software and systems development, Wayne is a CTO rock star!

He led AI-driven projects at bitHuman and AiFi, covering multimodal visual agents, autonomous checkout, synthetic data, and digital twin platforms.

He also holds 12 national patents in areas as diverse as smart home devices and computer vision!

DAVE SPILLANE

Chair of Board

Also hailing from Ireland, Dave holds a concurrent role at Catalyze Advisors and Point Arena LLC, where he helps run operations and finance.

Dave spent 12 years at Ernst & Young before joining Facebook as its Chief Accounting Officer.

After helping to take Facebook public in 2012, Dave joined Stripe as its Chief Accountant.
